Instrumentation Research Engineer

What

I work on the development, integration, test, validation and calibration of complex scientific instruments.
My work happens before an instrument is live and taking data, most of it even before the instrument is actually built.

With whom

Being involved from the hardware specification to the final data gives me the extraordinary opportunity to interact with scientists, engineers and technicians of various specialties : physics, astrophysics, biology, computing, electronics, mechanics.

Field

In the last 18 years, I've been working on big international astronomy projects, with a little detour in medical imaging.
Learn more in the Projects section.

Where

These projects have taken me to various research laboratories:
at CPPM in Marseilles, FR ;
at LAPP in Annecy, FR ;
at KIPAC in Stanford, CA ;
and currently at LPNHE in Paris, FR.
